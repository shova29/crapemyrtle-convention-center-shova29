EventSafe: Ensuring Safety During Weddings and other events

Tap on the EventSafe badge to see the hygiene & safety standards adhered by each event professional,
to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

Protective Gears

Routine use of masks, gloves and sanitizers are ensured by the professionals while at work. The WedMeGood team can conduct a cross check, asking for images to validate the usage of the mentioned items, from the professional at all times.

Staff Screening

Temperature screenings are conducted for the entire team on a daily basis.The temperature readings are then verified by the WedMeGood team via image before the execution of any booking.

Sanitization Services

Venues and Studios are undertaking numerous initiatives to disinfect their surfaces frequently. WHO guidelines have also been shared with the Professionals by the WedMeGood team for reference.

Social Distancing Norms

Professionals ensure to maintain at least 1 metre (3 feet distance) amongst themselves and others, while at work. They are also limiting the number of staff working at one time to ease distancing.

Planning an event can take from weeks to months,it really depends on the size and the details that the event involves. Having enough time allows you to comfortably cover different areas and prepare yourself for potential scenarios that might happen so you can consider various options prior the date. You always need more time than you think! However, when organising for a public event aiming at 100 to 1,000 attendees; the details should be released to the public and the media 2 months in advance. By positioning your event details correctly, people should be exposed to the information online and offline at least a few times before the event.
